Canadian Dishes

Poutine

One of the most famous french Canadian dishes to come out of canada, poutine is a delicacy enjoyed by many canadians which consists of fries, gravy and cheese curds. This dish was first developed as a side dish but nowadays it can me enjoyed as a whole meal as well.

Nanaimo Bars

A canadian dessert consisting of wafer, nut, and coconut crumb base, custard icing in the middle, and a layer of chocolate ganache on top. This dessert originates from Nanaimo, BC and is well loved by many Canadians.

Maple Syrup

A staple in Canadian Cuisine, maple syrup is made by taking maple sap out of maple trees. This sap is then evaporated and thickened into a syrup that we call maple syrup.

Tire d’érable sur la neige

Simliar to maple syrup, this dessert is made from maple sap but is evaporated past the point of being a syrup and is rapidly cooled over snow to create a maple taffy.
