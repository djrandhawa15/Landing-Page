Places to See

Niagara Falls

The most amazing waterfall in Canada, the combined falls have the highest flow rate of any waterfall in North America that has a vertical drop of more than 50 m (160 ft).

Toronto

Home of Drake, The Weeknd and Justin Beiber. This city is known as Canadian New York where the downtown area is very urban and houses the countries most famous attraction the CN Tower.

Vancouver BC

Known for it's Rocky Mountains, impressive ski resorts and is a major filming location for many hollywood shows and movies.

Montreal

Located in the french province of Canada, the main language in this region is french canadian and is modeled after old france with gothic architecture.

Nova Scotia

Located on the eastern part of Canada and is one of the 3 maritime provinces of Canada.
